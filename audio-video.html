Here I discuss about audio and video tags/elements

audio

The audio element is used to embed audio content in an HTML document without requiring any additional plug-in like Flash player.

To know audio tag attributes and values Click here

Audio attributes are autoplay, controls, loop, muted, preload, src


video

The video element is used to embed video content in an HTML document without requiring any additional plugin like Flash player.
Support for the video element varies across browsers. At the moment, there are three main video formats that is supported for the video element: MP4, Ogg and WebM.

To know video tag attributes and values Click here

Video attributes are autoplay, controls, loop, muted, preload, src width, height, poster
